Lassi

Ingredients

  • 2 cups curd
  • 1 cup water
  • 3 to 4 tablespoon sugar
  • Half teaspoon cardamon powder
  • 1 tablespoon chopped nuts

Preparation

  1. Add 2 cups chiled cured to a deep bowl or a blender/li>
  2. Whisk it well until smooth
  3. Next add 3 to 4 tablespoon sugar
  4. Add Half teaspoon cardamon powder
  5. Whisk everything well sugar dissolves
  6. Garnish with tablespoon chopped nuts
  7. Serve it chiled
